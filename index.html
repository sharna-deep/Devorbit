Mediseen

Mediseen is a medical service app where users can book doctor appointments, order medicines citywide, access ambulance and lab test services, and securely store medical records digitally—allowing doctors to easily view and manage patient history through the app.

Localfavs

Localfavs is a food delivery app that helps users discover and order popular local delicacies from their area. It highlights famous dishes from your locality, making it easy to explore, enjoy, and support regional food favorites with every order.

ClassTrack

ClassTrack is an attendance app for teachers to mark student presence digitally, eliminating the need for physical registers. It tracks weekly and monthly attendance, generating personalized reports for each student, making classroom management efficient and paperless.
